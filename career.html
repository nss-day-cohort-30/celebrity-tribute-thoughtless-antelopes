Career of Danny DeVito

Danny Devito was born in 1944 in Neptune, New Jersey. He moved to New York in 1962 to pursue a cosmetology career and quickly switched his main focus to acting. Shortly after his breakout role in 'One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest' in 1975, he moved to California. He and his wife, Rhea Perlman, currenty have two homes. One in Beverly Hills and one in Aspen, Colorado.

Danny DeVito has an illustrious career that spans 5 decades. He has worn every hat imaginable in show business with immense success. His skills do not end at acting and singing in front of the camera. He is also a celebrated director and producer.
The works below are only a few of the 4-500 credits he has accrued throughout his career.
